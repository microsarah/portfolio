Title: CLOUDS

----

Summary: An exploratory experience for an interactive documentary.

----

Role: experience design, visual design

----

Text:

I had the great honor to work in collaboration with a number of designers + developers on this truly unique interactive documentary. Creators James George and Jonathan Minard describe the film as "a portrait of this community of digital pioneers, explored through the lens of code. The project asks questions about the future of creativity at a time when algorithms play an important role in shaping culture."
