Title: CLOUDS

----

Summary: An exploratory experience for an interactive documentary.

----

Role: interaction & visual design

----

Text:

I had the great honor to work in collaboration with a number of designers and developers on this truly unique interactive documentary. Creators James George and Jonathan Minard describe the film as asking "questions about the future of creativity at a time when algorithms play an important role in shaping culture."

The film screened worldwide, and was awarded the <a href="https://tribecafilm.com/stories/here-are-your-tff-2014-award-winners" target="blank">Tribeca Film Festival Transmedia Award</a> and <a href="https://design100.com/NYC14/entry_details.asp?ID=12867" target="blank">NY Design Award</a> in 2014.

<a href="http://www.cloudsdocumentary.com/" target="blank">cloudsdocumentary.com</a>
